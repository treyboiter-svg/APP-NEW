Game-Time Air Density & Pressure

Open this dashboard each day to load scheduled games, then calculate game-time weather, true station pressure, and moist-air density at every venue.

Results use forecast/analysis values at the closest hourly timestamp to the scheduled start. “Station pressure” is absolute surface pressure—not sea-level pressure. Indoor/roofed venues are labeled and retain outdoor-at-venue weather, station pressure, and calculated density. Exact indoor density requires venue temperature/RH instrumentation.

A game schedule feed is an operational convenience; confirm time, venue, and roof status with the league or venue for professional wagering, safety, or performance decisions.
